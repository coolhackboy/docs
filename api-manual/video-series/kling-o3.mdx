<Tip>
1. After submission, a `task_id` will be returned. If you provided a `callback_url`, when the task status becomes `finished` or `failed`, a POST request will be sent to the `callback_url`.
2. Regardless of whether `callback_url` is provided, you can retrieve the response result through the unified [Query Task Status](/api-manual/task-management/status) endpoint.
</Tip>

## Available Models

- **kling-o3/standard** - Standard quality tier
- **kling-o3/pro** - Higher-quality pro tier

## Key Features

- **Three Modes**: Supports text-to-video, image-to-video, and reference-to-video
- **Reference Images**: Use `reference_image_urls` to enter reference-to-video mode, with up to 4 reference images
- **Image Anchors**: `image_urls` supports up to 2 images. In image mode they map to the main image and optional end frame; in reference mode they act as optional start and end anchors
- **Aspect Ratios**: `1:1`, `16:9`, and `9:16` are supported for text-to-video and reference-to-video. `aspect_ratio` is ignored in image-to-video
- **Multi-Shot**: Multi-shot storytelling with per-shot prompts and durations. When `multi_shots` is `true`, `multi_prompt` is required and `sound` must be `true`
- **Kling Elements**: `kling_elements` is only available in reference-to-video and is forwarded to the upstream `elements` field
- **Credits**: `kling-o3/standard` costs 10 credits/s without audio or 13 credits/s with audio. `kling-o3/pro` costs 13 credits/s without audio or 16 credits/s with audio
