# Sora 2 Video Generation

Generate professional-quality videos using OpenAI's Sora 2 model. Supports text-to-video and image-to-video generation with multiple quality tiers.

## Available Models

- **sora-2** - Standard quality video generation (10s, 15s)
- **sora-2-pro** - Premium quality with longer duration (15s, 25s)
- **sora-2-private** - Private deployment for standard quality
- **sora-2-pro-private** - Private deployment for premium quality

## Duration Options

| Model | Available Durations | Quality |
|-------|-------------------|---------|
| sora-2 | 10s, 15s | Standard |
| sora-2-pro | 15s, 25s | Premium HD |
